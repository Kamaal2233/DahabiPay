DahabiPay - Policy & Terms

1. Duplicate Orders

If a customer submits multiple orders using the same transaction, only the first valid order will be processed. Any additional submissions will be canceled automatically. If you believe there was an error, please contact our support team immediately.

2. Payment Time Limit

Orders must be paid within 15 minutes of being placed. If no payment is received within that time frame, the order will be canceled automatically for security and processing efficiency.

3. Proof of Payment

Customers are required to submit accurate proof of payment. Any fake or altered payment proof will result in account restriction or permanent ban.

4. Refund Policy

Refunds will only be issued if a genuine error has occurred on our end. If a customer sends funds to the wrong account or chooses incorrect options, DahabiPay is not responsible. Please double-check all details before submitting an order.

5. Exchange Rates

Our exchange rates are updated frequently and may change at any time without prior notice. The rate you see at the time of placing your order is what applies.

6. Fraud Prevention

We monitor all transactions for suspicious behavior. Any fraudulent activities will be reported to relevant authorities and may lead to account suspension.

7. Customer Support

If you face any issue during or after your transaction, our support team is available 24/7 to assist you. Please contact us through WhatsApp, Email or Telegram for quick response.
