Habraaca Sarifka DahabiPay

  1. Tag qaybta “Payments” oo dooro halka aad lacagta ka soo dirayso. Ka dibna ku dar lacagta account-ka aad dooratay adigoo copy dhahaya.
  2. Marka aad hubiso in lacagtu baxday, ku noqo page-kii sarifka oo tag “Exchange Now” kadibna buuxi xogaha soo socda:
    • Xaddiga aad dirtay
    • Magaca meesha aad ka soo dirtay
    • Magaca meesha aad u wado
    • Number-ka ama Hash-tag aad lacagta ka dirtay
    • Number-ka ama address-ka meesha aad lacagta u dirayso
    • Geli Email sax ah si aad ula socoto xaalada sarifkaaga
  3. Hubi xogtaada inaad sax buuxisay adigoo gujinaya “Submit ”.
  4. Intaas kadib waxaad heli doontaa fariin ku soo gaarta email-kaaga, taasoo kuu sheegaysa in lacag bixinta si guul leh u dhamaystiray.
Digniin: Haddii aad rabto in sarifkaagu si degdeg ah u dhaco, fadlan ha buuxin xogta adigoon lacagta dirin. Haddii kale, sarifka wuu dib u dhici karaa.
